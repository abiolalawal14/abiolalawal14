Data-Driven Insights on Regional Airline Profitability

Excel, SQL, Power BI

Embark on a journey into the aviation industry as this project uncovers valuable insights into the profitability of regional airlines across six global markets. Leveraging operational and financial data, the project focuses on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as EBIT, passenger yield, load factor, and fleet age to provide actionable insights. Through meticulous data preparation, analysis, and visualization, the project highlights the most profitable regions, efficiency gaps, and airline performance rankings. Armed with these insights, airlines and stakeholders can make informed decisions to optimize routes, improve yields, and modernize fleets, ultimately driving sustainable growth in an increasingly competitive market. Explore the power of data-driven recommendations in shaping the future of global aviation.
